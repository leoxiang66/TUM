Motivation

如何处理时序数据 (temporal sequential data) X1,X2,…,XTX_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{T}X1​,X2​,…,XT​？ e.g. XtX_{t}Xt​ = temperature on ttt-th day
  • 后面时刻的data依赖于前面时刻的data
  • data not i.i.d
  • the index ttt can correspond to time, location...
—> Autoregressive models (AR) 自回归模型

Assumption:
  • observations/data are continuous

  • timesteps are discrete

where
  • φ1,…,φp\varphi_{1}, \ldots, \varphi_{p}φ1​,…,φp​ are the parameters
  • ccc is a constant parameter
  • εt∼N(0,σ)\varepsilon_{t} \sim N(0, \sigma)εt​∼N(0,σ) is a white noise. (This is not a parameter, but a sample under the fixed normal distribution)
  • The variable Xt−iX_{t-i}Xt−i​ is the lagged value at time iii.
